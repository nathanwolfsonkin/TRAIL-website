TRAIL received a new award from NSF to research [Visual Tactile Neural Fields for Active Digital Twin Generation](https://www.nsf.gov/awardsearch/showAward?AWD_ID=2220866). This is a three-year collaboration with [Kostas Daniilidis](https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~kostas/) from the University of Pennsylvania and [Monroe Kennedy III](https://arm.stanford.edu/) from Stanford University.
